AIR ARRANGEMENTS

  • Air ticketing change fees will apply if changes are made after registration closes.
  • Pre/post extension fare differences are incurred at time of request and must be made through VZ Travel HQ prior to the registration deadline.
  • Your airline tickets and your government-issued photo ID must be in the same name. Please indicate the correct names for ticketing on the online registration site.

DRIVING ARRANGEMENTS

If you choose to drive to the Minneapolis, Minnesota area, you will be reimbursed for your miles driven, calculated from city to city (not door to door) using Verizon's current mileage reimbursement of 55 cents per mile. The dollar amount given shall not exceed the average ticket price from your destination city to the program city. All participants driving from the same center will each receive the same dollar amount. VZ Travel HQ must be notified at the time of enrollment that you choose to drive. If notification is not provided at the time, no driving reimbursement will be issued. All calculations will be determined prior to your trip by VZ Travel HQ and reimbursement will be given on-site.
